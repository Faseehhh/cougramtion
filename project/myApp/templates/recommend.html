A Bachelor of Science in Islamic Studies (BS Islamic Studies) is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on the academic study of Islam, its teachings, history, culture, and contributions to various fields. This program provides students with a deep understanding of Islamic theology, jurisprudence, philosophy, and ethics. BS Asian Studies

A Bachelor of Science in Asian Studies (BS Asian Studies) is an undergraduate degree program that focuses on the interdisciplinary study of the cultures, societies, languages, history, politics, and economies of Asian countries and regions. This program provides students with a deep understanding of Asia's diverse and complex dynamics, preparing them for careers in fields such as international relations, diplomacy, business, education, and research.
